Submissions


Please fill in the information below and submit your artwork. Make sure that the image you upload is either a JPEG, PDF, or HEIC. If there are any issues with your submission, we will email you to resolve the situation. In the description box, describe your artwork and explain the meaning behind the piece. Share your story! We want your voice to be heard through your artwork! Lastly, please acquire parental permission before releasing your artwork. Thank you!
